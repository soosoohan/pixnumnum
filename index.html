NumNum

What is NumNum?

NumNum is a strategic puzzle game where you drop number cards onto a grid and merge matching numbers together. Choose a column, drop your card, and merge your way to bigger numbers — no time pressure.

How to Play

Drop Cards

3 cards wait in queue. Choose a column to drop the first card — it lands on the bottom or on top of another card.

Merge Numbers

Matching numbers adjacent in any direction merge. The newer card is absorbed into the older one, doubling its value. Chain merges are possible.

Number cards (2, 4, 8, 16) and special cards are shuffled together randomly in the queue.

Special cards transform adjacent cards: 🍎×2 🍒×4 🍇×8

Numbers 2048 and above are not affected by fruit card effects.

Game Over

The game ends when the top row is full. Watch the top.

Controls

← → Select column (PC)
Enter, Space Drop card (PC)
Tap column button Drop card (mobile)
